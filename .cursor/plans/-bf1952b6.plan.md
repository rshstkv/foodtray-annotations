## Design Principles

**Visual Language:**

- Clean, minimal, focused (Linear, Vercel, Notion, Apple style)
- Typography: Inter or Space Grotesk, large headings, clear hierarchy
- Colors: Neutral grays, white backgrounds, ONE accent color (blue-600)
- Icons: lucide-react (Layers, Edit3, Trash2, etc.) - NO emoji
- Spacing: Generous whitespace, aligned to 8px grid
- Animations: Subtle, elegant (150ms ease transitions)

**UX Philosophy:**

- Design for focus and calm
- Every pixel has purpose
- Auto-detect when possible, don't ask
- One click > two clicks
- Primary action prominent, secondary as text link
- No Cancel buttons unless preventing data loss

**–ò–∑–±–µ–≥–∞—Ç—å:**

- Emoji
- Colorful badges everywhere
- Multiple borders/shadows
- Popups/modals (use inline panels)
- Multi-step forms
